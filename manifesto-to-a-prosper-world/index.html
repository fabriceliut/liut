Manifesto to a prosperous world.

What is a prosperous world you will ask me ?

This is a world we are all looking for. And you know why ? Simply because nature system is prosper, nothing is looking for an isolated grow. Everything is connected, we are connected, human to human, human to nature. We are parts of a big interconnected system, and it’s not a spiritual utopia, it’s a biological and physical fact.

We all breath the same (non so fresh) air. We need the same sunlight energy to feed us as vegetables, the same water to refresh our body and mind.

The Earth is our only home and our only boundary. No others boundaries needed. We need identities and diversity because this is the richness of life, this make us transform and evolve.

Prosperous life start by listening, observing, learning how an older and wiser system than us is working. Biomimicry is a science that can help us understand this system which we belong.

Design thinking propose us a “user centric” point of view. LIUT protocol research a “natural systemic thinking”. It use design thinking methods, but also systemic and circular design.

We need our attention and facilitators to Listen. We need a systemic and transdisciplinar approach to Interconnect ideas and generate evolutions. We need design thinking, design sprints and agility to Understand the complexity.

We need this LIUT open protocol to Transform our world to a prosperous, antifragile and resilient one.

LIUT is a community gathering human experts, facilitators, methods and protocols to transform our society to a prosperous playground.
